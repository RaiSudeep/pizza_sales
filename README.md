# 🍕🚀 **Pizza Sales Analysis Report**  
💾 **Data Processing: MySQL | Visualization: Python**    

## 🔗 Source: Kaggle Dataset  
**Pizza Sales Analysis Dataset**  
[🔗 Kaggle Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mysarahmadbhat/pizza-place-sales)  

---

## 📌✨ **1. Introduction**  
This project provides valuable insights into pizza sales performance, customer preferences, and revenue trends using SQL queries and Python visualizations. 
The goal is to generate actionable business intelligence that enhances decision-making.

### 📂📊 **Datasets Used:**  
📌 **Orders** – Order dates and times.  
📌 **Order Details** – Order quantities and pizza IDs.  
📌 **Pizzas** – Pricing and size details.  
📌 **Pizza Types** – Category and ingredient data.  

🛠 The analysis leverages **CTEs, Window Functions, Aggregations, and Python-based visualizations** to derive advanced insights.

---

## 🔥📈 **2. Business Questions & KPIs**  

This project answers ** SQL 14 key business questions**, categorized into **Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced levels**.

### 🟢📝 **Basic Level KPIs** 
|          **KPIs**                     | **Description**                                                              |
|---------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total Orders Placed**               | Total number of orders placed across the dataset.                            |
| **Total Revenue from Sales**          | Overall revenue generated by pizza sales.                                    |
| **Highest-Priced Pizza**              | Identifies the pizza with the highest price.                                 |
| **Most Common Pizza Size**            | Highlights the pizza size ordered most frequently by customers.              |
| **Top 5 Most Ordered Pizza Types**    | The most popular pizzas based on order quantities.                           |

### 🔵📊 **Intermediate Level KPIs**  
|          **KPIs**                     | **Description**                                                              |
|---------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Total Quantity by Category**        | Summarizes total orders grouped by pizza categories.                         |
| **Order Distribution by Hour**        | Shows the distribution of pizza orders throughout the day.                   |
| **Category-Wise Pizza Sales Distribution** | Revenue contribution percentages across categories.                         |
| **Average Pizzas Ordered Per Day**    | Calculates the daily average of pizzas ordered.                              |
| **Top 3 Most Ordered Pizza Types by Revenue** | Ranks pizzas by highest revenue contributions.                           |


### 🔴📌 **Advanced Level KPIs** 

|          **KPIs**                     | **Description**                                                              |
|---------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Revenue Contribution by Pizza Type** | Analyzes the share of revenue generated by each pizza type.                 |
| **Cumulative Revenue Over Time**      | Tracks how revenue accumulates steadily over time.                           |
| **Top 3 Most Ordered Pizzas by Revenue in Each Category** | Highlights category-specific revenue leaders.           |
| **Monthly Revenue Trends**            | Examines variations in revenue across months.                                |

## 📊🎨 **3. Python Visualizations and Insights**   

This project showcases the **11 Python-based visualizations** created to analyze and understand the data effectively.   

| **Visualization Topic**              | **Description**                                                               |
|--------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Most Common Pizza Size Ordered**   | Pie chart shows proportions of pizza sizes ordered.                           |
| **Top 5 Most Ordered Pizza Types**   | Line graph displays the popularity of the top 5 pizza types.                  |
| **Total Quantity Ordered by Pizza Category** | Bar chart compares sales quantities among pizza categories.                |
| **Order Distribution by Hour of the Day** | Hourly trends visualized through bar charts.                               |
| **Average Number of Pizzas Ordered Per Day** | Daily order trends captured in a line graph.                               |
| **Category-Wise Pizza Sales Distribution** | Donut chart illustrates category revenue contributions.                    |
| **Top 3 Pizza Types Based on Revenue** | Line graph highlights the revenue contributions of the top 3 pizzas.       |
| **Top 3 Pizza Types by Revenue for Each Category** | Grouped bar chart shows performance across pizza categories.          |
| **Monthly Revenue Trends**           | Bar chart depicts monthly revenue variations.                                 |
| **Cumulative Revenue Over Time**     | Line graph visualizes cumulative revenue growth day by day.                   |
| **Correlation Analysis**             | Scatter plot examines relationships between daily and cumulative revenues.    |  

**Visualization Tools Used: Matplotlib and seaborn**

## 📊🏆 **4. Expected Outcomes & Insights**  

By answering key business questions and creating visualizations:   

- Understand total orders and revenue trends, helping identify peak sales hours and the most popular pizza sizes.   

- Analyze top-performing pizzas by order quantity and revenue contribution to optimize menu offerings.   

- Evaluate the success of different pizza categories based on sales volume and revenue percentages.   

- Detect patterns in hourly, daily, and monthly sales for effective resource allocation and inventory management.   

- Examine cumulative revenue growth and correlation analysis to forecast business opportunities.
